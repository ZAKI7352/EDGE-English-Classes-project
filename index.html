Welcome EDGE English Classes

About Us

Founded in 2014 by Khalique Sir, a visionary educator, EDGE English Classes is the only institute in Bharwara dedicated to teaching spoken English with an emphasis on both grammar and conversation practice.
Beyond language skills, EDGE also provides comprehensive instruction for 11th and 12th-grade syllabus, Known for producing top scorers every year, EDGE English Classes focuses especially on students who need extra guidance, offering special classes to support and uplift them. Khalique Sir’s dedication and passion drive his commitment to student success.

Vision

At EDGE English Classes, our vision is to create a supportive learning environment that transforms how students perceive and approach English. We strive to make language mastery accessible for everyone in Bharwara, fostering skills that build confidence and open doors to future academic and career achievements. By focusing on practical language skills and individualized support, we envision a community where every student can achieve their fullest potential and contribute meaningfully to society.

Mission

Our mission at EDGE English Classes is to empower students with strong English communication skills, blending practical speaking exercises and foundational grammar. We focus on building confidence and academic excellence, especially for those who need extra support, by offering specialized classes and personalized guidance. With a commitment to holistic growth, we prepare students not only for exams but also for future success in their personal and professional lives.
